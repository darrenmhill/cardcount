## Description (4000 chars max)

Master the art of card counting with CardCount — the most comprehensive blackjack companion app for serious players and students of the game.

REAL-TIME COUNT TRACKING
Tap cards as they're dealt to track your running count and true count in real time. Supports 9 professional counting systems including Hi-Lo, Hi-Opt I, Hi-Opt II, Omega II, Zen Count, Wong Halves, KO, Red 7, and Ace-Five. Visual indicators show when the count is in your favor.

COMPLETE BASIC STRATEGY
Full basic strategy matrices for every hand combination, dynamically generated based on your specific table rules. Covers hard totals, soft totals, and pairs with color-coded actions: hit, stand, double, split, and surrender.

INDEX PLAYS & DEVIATIONS
The Illustrious 18 and Fab 4 surrender deviations are built in, plus additional index plays. Active deviations are highlighted based on the current true count so you always know when to deviate from basic strategy.

BETTING GUIDANCE
Smart bet spread recommendations based on the current count. Includes edge calculations, Kelly criterion sizing, and Wonging strategy tips. See exactly how many units to bet at every true count.

TRAINING DRILLS
Practice and sharpen your skills with speed counting drills, basic strategy quizzes, deviation tests, and true count conversion exercises. Track your accuracy and improvement over time.

BANKROLL MANAGEMENT
Calculate risk of ruin, recommended bankroll size, and optimal bet ramps using Kelly criterion. Visualize variance with a Monte Carlo simulation showing projected bankroll trajectories.

SESSION LOGGING
Track your casino sessions with results, duration, table conditions, and notes. Monitor your overall win rate and units per hour.

FULLY CONFIGURABLE
Customize every table rule: number of decks, dealer hits/stands on soft 17, double after split, surrender options, blackjack payouts, Charlie rules, deck penetration, and more. Choose from casino presets or dial in your exact conditions.

Built for education and practice. Designed with a premium dark theme inspired by the casino floor.
